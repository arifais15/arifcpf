# **App Name**: PBS CPF Compass

## Core Features:

- Chart of Accounts (COA) Management: Display and administer the given Chart of Accounts, including account types and normal balances, providing a foundational structure for all financial entries.
- Transaction Entry: Enable users to record all financial transactions (contributions, loan disbursements/recoveries, investments, income, expenses) accurately against the relevant accounts in the COA.
- Member Fund & Loan Tracking: Manage individual member contributions, PBS contributions, calculate cumulative interest, and track all CPF loan disbursements and their subsequent recoveries.
- Investment & Accrual Tracking: Monitor various investments (Fixed Deposits, Savings Certificates, Bonds, Shares) and automatically track accrued revenue interest based on system rules.
- Financial Reporting: Generate essential financial statements and reports, including ledgers, trial balance, income statements, and balance sheets, providing a clear overview of the fund's financial health.
- Transaction Entry Assistant: An AI tool that suggests appropriate account classifications and identifies potential data entry errors for newly entered transactions, enhancing efficiency and accuracy.
- Employee Subsidiary Ledger: Generate and display a detailed subsidiary ledger for individual employees, showing their provident fund contributions (employee and PBS), loan activities, accrued interest (profit), total fund, and cumulative fund balance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A professional and trustworthy blue (#24598F) for headings and key interactive elements, reflecting reliability in financial management.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue (#F0F2F4), providing a clean and understated canvas for financial data.
- Accent color: A deep, supportive indigo (#5252E0) for calls-to-action and important highlights, creating a calm yet effective contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, neutral, and highly readable characteristics, essential for data-heavy financial displays.
- Use a set of clear, concise, and professional icons that intuitively represent financial concepts and administrative actions, maintaining a serious and trustworthy aesthetic.
- Implement a structured, clean, and dashboard-centric layout with clear separation of sections and data tables to facilitate easy navigation and data interpretation for accounting professionals.
- Incorporate subtle and functional animations, such as gentle transitions for data updates or report generation, to enhance user experience without distraction.
